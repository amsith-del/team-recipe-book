# 🍗 Chicken Biriyani Recipe

## Ingredients
- Basmati rice
- Chicken
- Onion
- Tomato
- Ginger garlic paste
- Biriyani masala
- Salt
- Oil
- Water

## Steps
1. Wash and soak basmati rice
2. Fry onions until golden brown
3. Add ginger garlic paste and tomatoes
4. Add chicken and biriyani masala
5. Cook chicken properly
6. Add rice and water
7. Cook until rice becomes soft
8. Serve hot
